Who We Are?

Tormik Students Forum (TSF) is a vibrant and dynamic student organization established to empower the youth of Tormik Valley through education, skill development, and community engagement. Our mission is to create a platform where students can come together, exchange knowledge, and work collaboratively toward achieving academic and personal growth

Where We Are?
