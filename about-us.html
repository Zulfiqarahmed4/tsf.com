About Us

Tormik Student Federation is a vibrant community dedicated to empowering students through education, leadership, and collaboration. We provide opportunities for personal growth, academic excellence, and community engagement. Our mission is to inspire students to achieve their full potential and make a positive impact on society. Join us in creating a brighter future together!.

Who We Are?

Tormik Students Forum (TSF) is a vibrant and dynamic student organization established to empower the youth of Tormik Valley through education, skill development, and community engagement. Our mission is to create a platform where students can come together, exchange knowledge, and work collaboratively toward achieving academic and personal growth

Where We Are?

Tormik Students Forum (TSF) is based in the scenic Tormik Valley, a breathtaking region nestled in the heart of the mountainous district of Skardu, Gilgit-Baltistan. This valley lies at the crossroads of nature’s splendor, surrounded by towering peaks, lush greenery, and glacial streams, making it a serene and inspiring place for growth and learning
